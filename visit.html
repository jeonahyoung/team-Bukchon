Transportation Card in Korea

Step 1. Purchase Card

Purchase transoprtation card in any convienent store with deposit fee of 2,500 won, as well as in subway station.

Buy a transportation card at any convenience store or subway station.

Step 2. Deposit Cash

Ask to deposit the cash immediately to the staff. While 2500 won is required cash to deposit, for safety, 10000 won is recommended.

Load cash onto your card counter. 10,000 won is recommended.

Step 3. Using Subway/Bus

Simply tab your transportation card whenever it is required! You need to tab the card before riding subway or bus, and tab again after finishing your journey.

Tap your card before and after each ride on the subway or bus.

Step 4. Check Balance

You may need to visit the convienent stores or subway station to check your remaining balance in the card!

Check your card balance at any convenience store or subway station.
